Wellcome To GAC HYPER SSR Gallery

At a glance, the aerodynamic lines on this 2024 Hyper SSR give away its status as a serious car. The stunning red paint and butterfly doors don’t hurt in this regard. Its footprint is in the ballpark of a Lotus Evija, while its incredible acceleration rivals the mighty Rimac Nevera. Considering GAC Aion says it can run from 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in just 1.9 seconds and on to a top speed of 250 km/h (155 mph), it’s clear that this wild addition to the world of electric supercars is set to make an impression.
